BrickMMO LEGO Unit Converter

How does this tool work?

This tool is designed for converting measurements between millimeters, centimeters, inches, feet, and meters into Lego studs! Simply input the desired unit of measurement for length and width, and the tool will provide the corresponding number of studs for both dimensions. Additionally, it will suggest the required baseplates (48x48) and grids (16x16) to cover the desired area!
